Suthiporn Saipetch built the WeVoice - Sign Language app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by Suthiporn Saipetch at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use my Service. As used in this policy, “Personal Info” is data that can identify a particular person or device. Aggregate data isn’t considered Personal Info.

1. WeVoice - Sign Language never collects your Personal Info

The Services don't collect any of your Personal Info. Unlike most apps, WeVoice - Sign Language doesn't collect your IP address. I do detect non-personally identifiable geo-location information to optimize the services, but I definitely don't collect your precise geo-location or associate geo-location information with a particular user.

Because I do not collect information about your online activities over time and across third-party apps or online services, there is no need for us to respond to a browser’s Do Not Track settings, although I strongly support a consumer’s right to set such a preference and encourage all app operators to honor this consumer choice.

To improve the Services and your experience on the site, I may collect aggregate usage data from the Services and the app, including number of app views, visitor browser types, operating systems, or the links clicked to navigate to and from the site. I use a non-identifying first-party browser cookie to remember your search preferences (language, country, search engine).The cookies don’t track you. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or disable them, you can find out more or check your browser preferences.

2. WeVoice - Sign Language never sells your email address or any other Personal Info you volunteer

I don’t receive payment in cash or in kind from third parties in exchange for your Personal Info. Further, I don’t allow third parties to collect info about you on the site through cookies or other means.

3. WeVoice - Sign Language shares your Personal Info only in specific circumstances

There are a few, rare circumstances when I may have to share your Personal Info either to obey the law or protect the rights. I’ll share your Personal Info only to comply with laws or legally enforceable requests, to enforce my own rights and contracts with users or third parties, or to prevent harm to others and their property in an emergency situation.

In all instances, I’ll share the minimum info necessary to meet the immediate need and inform you of our disclosure when legally and practically possible.

4. Transparency report

As explained above, I will comply with a request for user data when the law requires it, but I require valid legal process to compel the disclosure of user data to the government; such as a legitimate and properly scoped court order, or a search warrant supported by probable cause and issued by an appropriate law enforcement authority. I interpret requests narrowly, and I will oppose unlawful or overbroad requests for specific user data.

Recipients of National Security Requests can only publish reporting bands instead of specific figures. If I receive such a request, I may challenge these reporting bands, in addition to opposing any unlawful or overbroad requests.
